My Career Goals

As a lover of art, strategy, and efficiency, my biggest dream is to work in a position where all of these elements can be combined. I specifically love video games and television and would love to be a marketing manager in either industry one day. My intrinsic motivations are to work on a team with passionate co-workers who deeply care about what they do, and share their work to inspire others.
